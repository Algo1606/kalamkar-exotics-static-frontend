About Kalamkar Exotics

Learn about our founder's vision and our company's journey

Our Founder

Mr.Sharad Kalamkar founded Kalamkar Exotics in 2003 with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between farming and the hospitality industry. With over 25 years of experience in agriculture, he recognized the need for a reliable, quality-focused vegetable supplier.

Our Journey

What started as a small family farm has grown into a comprehensive vegetable supply network serving over 100 restaurants, hotels, and cafes across the region. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer service has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to providing the freshest, highest-quality vegetables while supporting sustainable farming practices and building lasting partnerships with our clients. Every harvest is a step towards a more sustainable food system.
